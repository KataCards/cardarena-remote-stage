Bad gateway

The remote stage reached a gateway or proxy, but the upstream service behind it did not answer with a usable response. The frontend route may be healthy while a dependency is not.

What to check

  • Confirm the upstream API or proxy target is online and reachable.
  • Review gateway, reverse proxy, or service mesh logs for the failed handoff.
  • Retry once the dependent service is back in a healthy state.
